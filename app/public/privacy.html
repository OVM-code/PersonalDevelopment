What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and license key status (from Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider).
  • Usage data: monthly token counts, used to enforce the fair-use allowance.
  • Your courses: if you use "My courses" to save and resume a course, its title and full conversation content are stored on our servers under your account so you can access it from any device. You can delete any saved course yourself at any time from the "My courses" panel, which permanently removes it.

Where your messages go

Messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses, subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.

Payment data

Payments are processed entirely by Lemon Squeezy (merchant of record). We never see your card details.

Cookies

One functional cookie (cc_session) keeps you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies.
